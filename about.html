About Guild

Mission

Guild's mission is to enable small to medium sized web publishers to get paid fairly for their work. Advertising generates very small and unpredictable revenue streams for most publishers. Conventional subscription models only work for the very largest publishers. And donation-based approaches only work for publishers with an extremely loyal tight-knit following.

Inspiration

We looked far into the past and found a better way. Guilds were unions of craftsmen in the Middle Ages: stone cutters, saddle makers, etc. Each guild set its own standards and only those who met those standards were invited into the guild. People paid a premium for products and services from guild members because they were assured of the quality of the work. By organizing and maintaining high standards and consistent pricing, guild members were able to make an excellent living.

Guild for the modern web

Our Guild is the same thing for the modern web. Anyone can build a website. But we believe that consumers will pay fairly for access to the parts of the web that meet a set of standards for quality and performance and agree to eliminate advertising. The Guild sets these standards, establishes uniform pricing, and shares the proceeds among its members fairly.

Content

While we set standards for quality and performance, we do not seek to control content or aesthetics. Websites in the Guild may contain just about anything that provides value on the web -- including essays, news, videos, apps, art, tools, and so on.
