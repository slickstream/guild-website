How it works

Joining

To join the Guild, you register your site. You'll get a one-line embed code to add into your site, and designate the pages that will be added into the Guild. We will remain invisible to your visitors while we complete our review. If you are accepted, visitors landing on any of your designated pages will see that they are accessing a part of the Guild.

Experience

If your site's visitor has already paid for access, your page will have a small Guild certificate tab on it. Otherwise, we will present an overlay telling them that they are entering the Guild and inviting them to pay for access.

Depending on how you have configured your pages, we may or may not present them with a "skip" button to give each unique visitor access to a small amount of your content for free. We take care of tracking visitors to ensure that their free access is properly limited.

A compelling story

Our challenge is to tell a compelling story to visitors and make a purchase easy and quick. We have found that most users are very happy to support content from publishers who hold themselves to high standards. And using modern web payment technology, completing a payment transaction is simple, transparent, and fast.

Revenue

As a member, you earn a share the proceeds from paying visitors to all sites within the Guild. All combined payments (net of bank transaction fees) go into a single pot. We take a 15% fee to run the network. The remainder of the pot is distributed to members based on two factors: conversions and engagement.

Conversions are a measure of how many visitors pay for Guild access on a paywall erected by us on your site. Engagement is a measure of how much content is consumed by paying visitors to your site. Your earnings are distributed to you monthly. You can track your progress and history on the Guild website.
